Title: Uber Operational & Revenue Performance Dashboard
Project Overview
This project transforms fragmented ride-sharing data into a strategic BI tool. It provides a 360-degree view of business operations, focusing on revenue leakage, vehicle efficiency, and urban mobility patterns.

Key Features:

Executive Overview: At-a-glance tracking of core KPIs, including Completed vs. Lost Bookings, Total Revenue, and Average Trip Distance.

Revenue Analytics: Detailed breakdown of earnings by Vehicle Type and Payment Method with Monthly and Quarterly trend analysis.

Operational Heatmaps: Identification of "Busy Time Slots" and "Busy Areas" to highlight supply-demand gaps.

Rider Segmentation: Visualization of rider loyalty tiers (First-time, Return, and Regular) and analysis of cancellation reasons.

Technical Highlights

Tool: Power BI

Data Modeling: Established relationships between bookings, vehicle types, and customer segments.

Advanced Logic: Implemented filters for Monthly and Quarter-over-Quarter performance tracking.

Title: Spotify Global Top 50 - Audio Feature & Popularity Analysis
Project Overview
This project analyzes the "Top 50 - Global" dataset to uncover the underlying musical patterns that drive track popularity. It bridges the gap between technical audio engineering metrics and commercial success.

Key Features:

Global KPI Tracking: Real-time visualization of average track popularity and artist representation.

Musical Anatomy: Analysis of technical audio features such as Loudness, Liveness, Acousticness, and Speechiness.

Tempo & Mood Correlation: Investigation into how BPM, Energy, and Valence (musical positiveness) influence a song's position in the charts.

Artist & Genre Leaderboards: Identification of the most influential genres and artists currently dominating the global landscape.

Technical Highlights

Environment: Power BI.

Analysis: Correlation matrices between energy and danceability to identify "hit" characteristics.

Visualization: Distribution plots of BPM and genre-wise popularity breakdowns.
