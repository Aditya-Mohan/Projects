1. Project Overview:

This project transforms fragmented ride-sharing data into a strategic Business Intelligence tool. It addresses key operational challenges such as revenue leakage from lost bookings and identifying geographic demand surges.


2. Key Dashboard Pages:

Executive Overview: Real-time tracking of total revenue and booking success rates with interactive monthly and quarterly filters.

Vehicle Performance: Granular breakdown of booking counts and revenue contribution percentages by vehicle category.

Revenue Deep-Dive: Analysis of financial flow by payment method (Cash vs. Digital) and customer loyalty tiers.

Rider Insights: Investigation into cancellation reasons and rider frequency to improve the user experience.

Logistics & Mapping: Visualization of total distance traveled and identification of "Busy Areas" to visualize urban mobility patterns.


3. Technical Stack:

Data Visualization: Power BI

Data Modeling: DAX for measures like "Contribution %".

Analysis: Trend Analysis, Geospatial Mapping, and Customer Segmentation.
